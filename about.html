About Us

News Café is more than just a coffee & cocktail bar, restaurant & entertainment venue. It’s a trendsetting & benchmarking brand within the industry.

Our Story

Founded in 1995 as part of the Fournews group, News Cafe began in Hatfield and has grown into a global brand over the years. Now, three decades later, we remain at the forefront of food, cocktails, and social experiences. As a proudly South African brand, we’ve expanded our presence across several African nations, revolutionizing the industry with creativity and flair.

The Concept

News Cafe is not just a place, it’s a full experience. Combining the best of a coffee and cocktail bar, a top-tier restaurant, and a vibrant entertainment spot, we set the benchmark for the industry.

We blend modern dining with a casual hangout vibe. From handcrafted coffee to signature dishes, every element is curated to offer a full-sensory café experience.

Formula for Success

Passion. Commitment. Consistency. These are the core values that have established News Cafe as South Africa's leading entertainment hotspot.

With our innovative marketing strategies and strong brand collaborations, we keep our venues alive with energy, ensuring continued growth and an ever-changing experience for our guests.

Welcome to News Cafe – where every visit is a vibe.

The Vibe

Our bold store designs, award-winning mixology, and culturally forward music embody the distinctive News Cafe vibe. With cocktails crafted with flair and a world-class food menu, we ensure every experience is exceptional. Our energetic team matches the vibrant atmosphere we’ve built, making every visit unforgettable.
