Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered with a shot of espresso.

What are your opening hours?

We’re open daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, including weekends and public holidays.

Do you have vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes! We offer a range of vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free items clearly marked on our menu.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Absolutely. Ask any of our staff for the password when you dine in.

Can I host a private event at News Café?

Yes, our café space is available for private bookings. Contact us for details and availability.

Can I bring my own cake for a celebration?

Yes, you can bring your own cake for special celebrations. Please notify us in advance if you're planning to do so.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, debit/credit cards, and mobile payment apps like Apple Pay and SnapScan.

Do you offer takeaway?

Yes, you can order takeaway in-store.

Do you have a loyalty program?

We do! Join our loyalty program for exclusive discounts and offers. Ask our staff for more details.
